Active Programs

Every program is hands-on, competition-ready, and matched to your child's age and level.

🤖
Robotics
Design, build, and code competition robots from the ground up. Students learn mechanical design, wiring, and programming while working as a team toward a real competition goal.
Grades 4–12
🥇
FLL — First Lego League
Teamwork meets LEGO robotics and real-world research. Younger students design, build, and program a LEGO robot while tackling an annual research challenge together.
Grades 2–8
🏆
FTC — First Tech Challenge
Competition robotics for older students. FTC teams build and code metal-and-motor robots, then compete in regional and state tournaments against schools across the region.
Grades 7–12
🚁
Drone
Pilot, program, and race real UAV hardware. Students master flight controls, autonomous programming, and the engineering behind drones — then put their skills to the test.
Grades 5–12
📐
CAD
Computer-Aided Design and 3D modeling. Students turn ideas into precise 3D models, learning the design tools real engineers use every day in product and mechanical design.
Grades 6–12

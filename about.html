About STEM4U

Real tools. Real teams. Real engineers.

Our Mission

STEM4U is a K-12 STEM enrichment center in Northern Virginia built around real hands-on engineering — real tools, real teams, real engineers. We give kids the workshop, the mentorship, and the challenge they need to build things that actually work.

Our Approach

We believe kids learn engineering by doing engineering. Every program is hands-on and competition-ready, not screen-based theory. Students design, build, wire, code, and test — and when something breaks, they figure out why and fix it. That's where real learning happens.

We're mentors, not marketers. We respect our students as capable young engineers, meet them at their age and level, and push them toward real goals: a robot that scores, a drone that flies a clean course, a design that prints. We prepare FLL and FTC teams for regional and state competition, and we build the kind of confidence that carries beyond the workshop.

Where We Are

STEM4U is rooted in Northern Virginia, founded by local parents who wanted better, hands-on STEM options close to home. We're opening soon — join the waitlist to be first to know when enrollment opens and to lock in early access.
